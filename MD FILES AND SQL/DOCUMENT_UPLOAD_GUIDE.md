## How to Upload Documents

### Option 1: Upload from Knowledge Base Detail Page

1. **Navigate to a Knowledge Base**
   - Click **"Knowledge"** in the sidebar
   - Click on any KB (or create a new one)

2. **Click "Upload Document" Button**
   - Located in the top-right of the Documents section
   - Opens a modal dialog

3. **Upload Your File**
   - **Drag & Drop:** Drag your file onto the upload area
   - **Browse:** Click "browse files" to select from your computer
   - Supported formats: **PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT**
   - Maximum size: **50MB**

4. **Wait for Processing**
   - Progress bar shows upload status
   - Documents are automatically processed
   - Chapters are extracted automatically

5. **Done!**
   - Modal closes automatically
   - Your document appears in the KB

---

### Option 2: Upload from Empty State

If a KB has no documents yet:
1. You'll see an **"Upload First Document"** button
2. Click it to open the upload modal
3. Follow steps 3-5 above

## Troubleshooting

### "Upload failed"
- **Check file size** - Must be under 50MB
- **Check file type** - Only PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT supported
- **Try again** - Temporary server issues can be resolved with retry
- **Check connection** - Ensure stable internet connection

### "No chapters extracted"
- **Text-based PDFs work best** - Scanned images may not extract well
- **Try DOCX format** - Sometimes extracts better than PDF
- **Add chapters manually** - You can generate lessons from the full document

### "Document not appearing"
- **Refresh the page** - Sometimes needs a refresh
- **Check KB selection** - Ensure you're looking in the right KB
- **Try adding manually** - Go to KB â†’ "Add Document" to add it

### Slow upload
- **Large files take longer** - Be patient with 20+ MB files
- **Stable connection required** - Pause other downloads
- **Check server status** - Backend may be processing other uploads

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I upload the same document to multiple KBs?**
A: Yes! Upload once, then add to as many KBs as needed.

**Q: What happens to my uploaded files?**
A: They're stored securely in Supabase and only accessible to you.

**Q: Can I delete uploaded documents?**
A: Yes! Click the trash icon to remove from a KB or delete permanently.

**Q: How long does processing take?**
A: Usually 5-30 seconds depending on document size and complexity.

**Q: Can I upload images or videos?**
A: Not yet - currently only text documents are supported.
